# How AI Forecasting Beats Spreadsheet Models for Subscription Apps

Every subscription app founder has the same Google Sheet. New subscribers per month, churn rate, ARPU, projected MRR. The formula is elegant. The output is wrong.

## Why Spreadsheets Fail

A spreadsheet forecast assumes three things that are never true:

1. Your churn rate is constant
2. Your acquisition cost is constant
3. The future looks like the past

In reality, churn spikes after pricing changes. CAC drifts upward as you exhaust your best channels. Seasonality shifts your entire economics in Ramadan and again in summer.

A spreadsheet cannot see any of this. An AI model trained on millions of subscription cohorts can.

## What Good Forecasting Looks Like

Good forecasting is not one number. It is three.

- **Best case**: What happens if your current trajectory accelerates
- **Likely**: What happens if nothing changes
- **Worst case**: What happens if your highest-risk metric breaks

Each scenario has a confidence interval. Each is updated daily as new data arrives. Each comes with the specific actions that move you from worst case toward best case.
